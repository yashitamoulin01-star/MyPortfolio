WishyBloom

E-commerce Aggregation Node.js • PostgreSQL • Puppeteer

The Challenge

Plant enthusiasts face a highly fragmented market. Rare plants are sold across hundreds of boutique, independent Shopify stores, making inventory tracking and price comparison impossible. WishyBloom aggregates this data into a unified storefront.

System Architecture

The aggregation engine is built on a distributed cluster of Node.js workers running Puppeteer and Cheerio. These workers systematically scrape partner sites on a cron schedule, normalizing varying schema data into a strict unified product model stored in PostgreSQL.

Business Impact

WishyBloom scaled to 10k+ early active users, serving as the central hub for rare plant discovery. Our unified checkout flow drove a 40% conversion lift for our partner retail stores.
